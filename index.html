About us

Climo Studios is a small commune of local designers in the Wellington area, although we can do work worldwide. The aim for Climo Studios was first to make a portfolio website for me (Jason Climo) but then spread out to be able to subcontract other designers that I know to strengthen the types of design we can do and so far we can do: Web Design, Graphic Design: logos, illustrations, analytics or any computer-based graphics, Print-based work such as magazines, posters, business cards, stickers, anything you want that can be printed! We now have an in-house motion graphics designer which now opens us up to small scale animations, and screen overlays.

We aim to be an affordable solution for small companies, start-up businesses and individuals wanting our services. We want our customers to be as happy as we are as this is our passion and we can't wait to be contracting for you :)
